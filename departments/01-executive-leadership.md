## Why
`claw-empire` is a "CEO Desk" — command your agent empire from one place (local-first, orchestrates
CLI + OAuth + agents). `markus` is an "OS for AI workforces" with cross-session memory. `auto-company`
runs 14 role-agents (Bezos/Munger/DHH…) in a 24/7 loop. CrewAI gives you a clean `CEO` role agent.

## Integration

high-impact decisions to the human owner.
