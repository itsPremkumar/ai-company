# E-Commerce (Online Store / Digital Sales)

**Role:** Sell products and services online — storefront, checkout, order management, payments, inventory.

## Recommended projects
| Project | ★ | License | Link |
|---|---|---|---|
| `medusajs/medusa` | 35.1k | MIT | https://github.com/medusajs/medusa |
| `saleor/saleor` | 22k+ | BSD-3 | https://github.com/saleor/saleor |
| `vendure-ecommerce/vendure` | 6k+ | MIT | https://github.com/vendure-ecommerce/vendure |

## Why
Medusa is the strongest open-source Shopify alternative — modular, self-hostable, with a built-in framework for customization. Saleor offers a GraphQL-native approach. Vendure is TypeScript-first. All three support multi-currency, multi-channel, and headless architecture.

## Integration
E-commerce storefront (Medusa) → connected to ERPNext for inventory/fulfillment → NocoBase for order CRM → Mautic for marketing → n8n for workflow automation.
