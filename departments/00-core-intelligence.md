## Why this is the "brain"
Without persistent memory + knowledge graph, agents are amnesiac. Mem0 (memory) + Graphiti (graph) +
pgvector (vectors) + Open WebUI (interface) + Docling (parsing) + SearXNG (private search) = a
self-improving, recall-capable AI workforce. This is the differentiator between a toy agent and an
enterprise AI company.
